**Format:** Wednesday nights • 2 hours • Knoxville, TN • No cost

- Direct teaching (no fluff)
- Real discussion
- Weekly actions you actually do
- Brotherhood and accountability

## The outcomes we fight for
- Identity and conviction
- Responsibility without shame
- Strong boundaries and clean integrity
- Marriage and family leadership
- Purpose and mission
